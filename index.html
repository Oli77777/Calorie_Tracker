Pumpkin Soup ingredients cook it
  • pumpkin 200g
  • white onions 30g
  • celeriac 30g
  • carrots 30g
  • ginger 10g
  • coconut milk 200g
  • vegetable stock 200g
  • honey or maple sirup
  • salt, cayenne pepper
  • olive oli 5g
  1. cut all vegetables in dices about 1cm
  2. heat olive oil in a pot
  3. saut√© the vegetables until softened but no colour
  4. add honey/maple sirup and let caramelize
  5. deglaze with the stock
  6. add coconut milk, reduce heat
  7. let gently boil for about 25 min.
  8. add salt/pepper to taste
  9. blend and serve
  10. optional pumpkin seeds, sprouts, cream
stuffed eggpland ingredients cook it
  • 2 eggplants cut in halves
  • white onions diced 20g
  • cherrie tomates cut in cuarters 50g
  • grated parmiggiano cheese 10g
  • 2 garlic cloves chopped
  • 1 tbsp. rosemary, thyme, parsley each, chopped
  • tomato paste 20g
  • vegetable stock 50g
  • olive oil 5g
  • salt, pepper
  1. take out eggplant pulp and cut into 1cm dices
  2. heat olive oil in a flat pan
  3. add all vegetables and cook until softened
  4. add tomato paste, stock and reduce until almost dry
  5. add chopped herbs and adjust seasoning to taste
  6. put the cooked vegetable mix in the eggplant
  7. top with the grated cheese
  8. bake in preheated oven at 150 C for 25 Minutes.
